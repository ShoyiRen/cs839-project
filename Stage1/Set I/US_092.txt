World champion Jacqueline Loelling of <L>Germany</L> leads after two runs of four in the women's skeleton competition at the Olympic Sliding Centre in <L>Pyeongchang</L>.
Loelling leads by 0.02 seconds from Austria's Janine Flock with Britain's Olympic champion Lizzy Yarnold 0.10 secs back in third.
Another Briton Laura Deas is fourth with Germans Anna Fernstaedt and Tina Hermann rounding out the top six.
The last two runs will take place Saturday.
