Jocelyne Lamoureux-Davidson set a new Winter Olympics record on Tuesday, scoring the two fastest back-to-back goals in Games history, as Team <L>USA</L> ran out comfortable 5-0 winners against the Olympic Athletes from <L>Russia</L>.
Her two strikes in the second period, only six seconds apart, beat the previous record of eight seconds set by Sweden's Carl Goran Oberg in 1960 at Squaw Valley.
