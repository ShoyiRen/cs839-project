The Russian duo of Anastasia Bryzgalova and Aleksandr Krushelnitckii have won the Bronze medal match against Norway's Kristin Skaslien and Magnus Nedregotten.
Three consistent ends to finish up the match put it beyond the Norwegians. The match finished 8-4.
The Gold medal match, between <L>Canada</L> and <L>Switzerland</L>, takes place this evening, at 8:05 p.m. local time.
