Matt Dalton was terrific in goal for <L>South Korea</L>. Dalton hails from <L>Clinton</L>, <L>Ontario</L>, and is one of six born-and-raised Canadians on coach Jim Paek's team. Paek was born in <L>Seoul</L>, <L>South Korea</L> but moved to <L>Canada</L> with his family when he was one.

He played junior for the Oshawa Generals and helped them win the 1986-87 <L>Ontario</L> Hockey League championship. He also spent a season with the Canadian national team before joining Mario Lemieux and the <L>Pittsburgh</L> Penguins in time for their back-to-back Stanley Cup championships in 1990-91 and 1991-92.
