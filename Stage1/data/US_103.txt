No cross-country skier in history had won the same individual individual event three Olympics in a row ... until today.
Switzerland's Dario Cologna crossed the line in 33:43.90 to win his third straight gold in the men's 15km, finishing 18.30 seconds ahead of Norway's Simen Hegstad Krueger who earned silver.
Rising star Denis Spitsov, 21, claimed the bronze medal for the Olympic Athletes from <L>Russia</L>.
