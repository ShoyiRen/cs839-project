The <L>Netherlands</L> absolutely dominated the speed skating at <L>Sochi</L> 2014, winning 23 of their 24 medals in the discipline (the other medal came in short track speed skating).
If the women's 3,000m speed skating is anything to go by, it looks as though that trend is going to continue in <L>Pyeongchang</L>.
Carlijn Achtereekte (C) claimed the gold medal, narrowly edging out defending Olympic champion Ireen Wust (L) in second and Antoinette De Jong (R), who completed the Dutch one-two-three.
